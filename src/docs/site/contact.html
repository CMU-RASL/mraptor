Microraptor was developed jointly by Trey Smith and Brennan Sellner, with some input from Chris Urmson in the initial planning stage. For the most part, Trey developed the daemon (mraptord) and text client (mrterm), while Brennan built the graphical client interface (claw).

For the time being, Brennan is the Microraptor point of contact, and can be reached via email at 'brennan' 'plus-sign' 'mraptor' 'at' 'ri.cmu.edu'.
